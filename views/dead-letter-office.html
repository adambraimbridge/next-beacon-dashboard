Dead Letter Office

Sometimes pages can't be found. How many next.ft.com URLs lead to a dead letter office?

Timeframe: 14 days | 30 days | 60 days | 90 days

Error pages

When a page can't be loaded, next.ft.com shows an error page. We can track this via a page.location.pathname of "errors/page". This page on Wikipedia will tell you what the different error numbers mean.

Broken URLs

The page.referrer.pathname is the URL that triggered the page.location.pathname (that is, the page that triggered the error).
