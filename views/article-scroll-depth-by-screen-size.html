Article Scroll Depth by Screen Size

What was the distribution of maximum depth that a reader got to in articles grouped by screen size.
WARNING: data in development and not verified!
Scroll depth tracking is fired off when the 25%, 50%, 75% and 100% points of the article body (excludes more on's) are displayed on the users screen. For a short article on a big screen, all tracked points may be visible at once. This will bias the results for large screens towards 100%.

Timeframe: 14 days (default) | 30 days | 60 days | 90 days
