We want to track how many people open the tray and then consume an article from it

Definitions
    Seen the tray: A user is defined as having potentially seen the tray, if they are following at least one topic and have visited pages that are not the homepage.
    Open Rate: Of those who may have seen the tray, how many went on to open them.
    Click Through Rate: Of those who opened the tray, how many clicked on a link inside it.
